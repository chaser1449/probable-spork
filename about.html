About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to support and empower parents navigating the painful challenges of parental alienation by offering accessible resources, emotional guidance, and a community of understanding. We strive to reunite families, restore relationships, and advocate for change through education, support, and awareness.

Who We Are

Parental Alienation Support is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping parents affected by the emotional and legal struggles of parental alienation. Founded with compassion and personal experience, our organization exists to provide clarity, community, and credible resources for those feeling isolated and unheard.

What We Offer

  • Expert-vetted articles and resources
  • Connections to therapists, legal advocates, and support groups
  • Awareness campaigns to educate the public and policymakers
  • A digital community rooted in empathy, strength, and hope
